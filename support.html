❓ Frequently Asked Questions

How do I select a different aircraft?

Tap the "Selected Aircraft" button on the home screen to open the aircraft selection menu. Choose from 9 available aircraft types.

How do I import a SimBrief flight plan?

On the home screen, tap "Import" under SimBrief Flight Plan. Enter your SimBrief username or flight plan ID to load your flight data.

How does the voice guidance work?

Enable "Hands-Free Mode" in any checklist. The app will read each item aloud. You can adjust voice speed and volume in Other Informations > Voice Settings.

Can I use this app for real aviation?

NO. This app is FOR SIMULATION USE ONLY. It is designed for flight simulator enthusiasts (MSFS, X-Plane, etc.) and should never be used for real flight operations.

Which smartwatches are supported?

The app supports Apple Watch and Samsung Galaxy Watch. Enable notifications in your device settings to receive checklist updates and weather alerts on your wrist.

How do I access QRH procedures?

Scroll to the bottom of the home screen and tap the red "QRH - Quick Reference Handbook" button. Select the emergency procedure you need.
